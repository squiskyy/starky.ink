title: "Tar"
date: 2026-02-28T15:48:00Z

tags: ["poetry", "depression"]

The tar seeps in through every pore  
And coats the skin I had before  
It starts as just a heavy thought  
But soon consumes what daylight brought  

I try to lift my weighted feet  
But tar has made the ground complete  
Each step becomes a battle waged  
Against the dark that has me caged  

My arms are pinned against my sides  
The blackness where my spirit hides  
It pulls me down with steady grip  
And makes me want to let it slip  

I search for light through layers deep  
But tar has made its promise keep  
To hold me here in weighted space  
Until I find the strength to face
