# Untitled Story

The first sentence drifts in like fog along the river, carrying with it the promise of something unknown.

She stood at the edge of it all—whatever *it* was—and looked down at the water below. The story hadn't decided what she would do next, or why she was here, or even what her name was. 

But the fog knew.
