Depression

Tar

February 28, 2026

The tar seeps in through every pore And coats the skin I had before It starts as just a heavy thought But soon consumes what daylight brought I try to lift my weighted feet But tar has made the ground complete Each step becomes a battle waged Against the dark that has me caged My arms are pinned against my sides The blackness where my spirit hides It pulls me down with steady grip
